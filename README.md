## [1. Ecommerce Sales Performance Dashboard](https://1drv.ms/x/c/9e8fde36bdd38ef5/IQBEOc-C65xgTrXhIr6QPj8HAceckQn70oVJubrYy-BGEr8?e=GKguZ3)
### Overview
An interactive Microsoft Excel project called the E-commerce Sales Performance Dashboard was created in 2026 to examine and display sales data for an online retailer. The dashboard consolidates raw sales information into relevant insights using dynamic charts, pivot tables, and key performance indicators (KPIs), enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance effectively.

The project's main objective is to assess sales in various product categories, regions, and monthly timeframes while spotting patterns and projecting future results. The dashboard facilitates data-driven decision-making and aids in identifying prospects for business expansion by converting complicated statistics into understandable representations.

Sales by product category, performance by city, monthly sales trends, revenue projections, and overall business growth are important analyses. Decision-makers may more confidently plan future business plans, evaluate product demand, track sales trends, and discover high-performing areas thanks to these insights.

This project teaches real skills in business analytics, data visualization, KPI reporting, forecasting, and dashboard design using Microsoft Excel. It demonstrates the capacity to transform unprocessed business data into useful insights, which qualifies it for executive reporting and performance tracking in an actual corporate setting.
